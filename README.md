 <h1>🌞 Solar Land Acquisition Analysis</h1>

<p>
An analytical project designed to track land acquisition progress for solar energy projects, 
focusing on identifying gaps, delays, and operational inefficiencies across multiple projects.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Short Description</h2>
<p>
This project provides a structured view of land acquisition stages—from identification to payment completion—
helping stakeholders monitor progress, detect bottlenecks, and improve decision-making in project execution.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🛠️ Tech Stack</h2>
<ul>
  <li>📊 Microsoft Excel – Data cleaning, KPI calculation, and dashboard creation</li>
  <li>📈 Data Visualization – KPI cards, comparison charts, and funnel analysis</li>
  <li>📂 Data Structuring – Organized project and parcel-level datasets</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📂 Data Source</h2>
<p>
Internal structured dataset containing:
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Project-level land requirements</li>
  <li>Parcel-level acquisition data</li>
  <li>Agreement, registration, and payment stages</li>
  <li>District and project identifiers</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Features & Highlights</h2>

<h3>🔍 Business Problem</h3>
<p>
Land acquisition is a multi-stage process involving identification, agreements, registration, and payments.
However, there is limited visibility into progress and bottlenecks across projects.
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Difficulty tracking acquisition progress</li>
  <li>Unclear identification of delays</li>
  <li>Lack of visibility into required vs secured land</li>
</ul>

<h3>🎯 Goal of the Project</h3>
<p>
To build an analytical solution that:
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Tracks end-to-end land acquisition progress</li>
  <li>Highlights gaps and bottlenecks</li>
  <li>Enables comparison across projects</li>
  <li>Supports data-driven decision making</li>
</ul>

<h3>📈 Key Analysis Performed</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>KPI Analysis:</b> Total Land Required, Land Identified, Land Secured, Progress %, Parcels, Payments</li>
  <li><b>Land Gap Analysis:</b> Comparison between required and secured land across projects</li>
  <li><b>Process Funnel Analysis:</b> Tracking drop-offs from agreement to payment stages</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>💡 Key Insights</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Significant gap observed between required and secured land</li>
  <li>Major drop between agreement completion and registration stages</li>
  <li>Payment inconsistencies affecting project timelines</li>
  <li>Variation in performance across projects indicating execution differences</li>
</ul>
